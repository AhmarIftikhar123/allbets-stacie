Baseball

Total Bases (Hits Only) -Single = 1, Double = 2, Triple = 3, Home Run = 4, Walks Do Not Count. College Baseball World Series – Bottom Team Does Not Necessarily Bat Last. World Baseball Classic – Games That Are Called Due To Mercy Rule, Run-lines & Totals Are Considered No Action.
